Diane Njuguna, Deputy Head of Social Media and Online Marketing


No work day is regular for Diana. She arrives at work each day bubbling with freshly juiced creativity, her unexplainable love for music and the most eclectic songs playlist.
At RADP Kenya, Diane is the office DJ.

She may be Deputy Head of Social Media and Online Marketing, but work is not enough to hold back this melophile from entertaining her squad with amazing tunes.

Diane is a sweet soul from the town of Nakuru in Kenya. Fascinated by how the digital media space has transformed communication and made it much more interesting, she joined RADP a little less than two years ago. She is grateful that RADP has given her the opportunity to channel her creativity and she is grateful for teammates that fuel her passion and give her confidence to face any challenge.

There are many reasons why RADP is a special place for Diana, but guess what’s the most interesting of all?

The opportunity to meet some of the most good-looking people ever!

How cool is that!

Please, when next you’re having a bad day, pat yourself on the back and remind yourself you are one of the pengest persons in someone’s life.

Apart from meeting beautiful people, Diana does her bit to make the world a beautiful place. She loves kids and volunteers as a caregiver and dance instructor in children homes.

She hopes to become a digital media expert, travelling the world, making memories while consulting for individuals and corporate organizations on how to set up an effective social media team.

But for now, she’s following celebrity news and letting down her hair on weekends to attend the latest entertainment events.
